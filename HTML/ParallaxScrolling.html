Parallax Scrolling

About Parallax

Parallax scrolling is a web design effect where the background moves at a slower pace than the foreground, creating an illusion of depth.

History of Parallax

The parallax effect was first used in traditional animation and video games to create a sense of depth by moving background layers at different speeds.

Parallax in Web Design

Modern websites use parallax scrolling to enhance visual storytelling, making web pages more engaging and interactive.

Benefits of Parallax

Parallax scrolling improves user experience by adding depth and motion, making content more dynamic and visually appealing.
